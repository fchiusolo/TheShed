About The Shed

The Shed is a place where software tools are built to solve common problems.

Ideas usually start small: noticing something that could work better, taking it apart, and trying to put it back together in a simpler way. Projects focus on turning those ideas into tools that are practical, focused, and ready to be used.

The Shed exists for the joy of building things that work. It approaches problems with curiosity, experimentation, and a hands-on mindset, aiming to create solutions that feel thoughtful and straightforward.

What matters is making something useful — and enjoying the process of making it.
