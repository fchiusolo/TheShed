Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 2025

HomeOps respects your privacy. This app is designed to work fully offline and does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

Data Collection

The app stores all user-entered information exclusively on the device locally, ensuring no external transmission occurs.

Accounts & Tracking

The service operates without requiring user registration, analytics implementation, or incorporation of third-party tracking software and SDKs.

Notifications

Any notifications generated by HomeOps are created and delivered locally on your device. No server communication is involved.
